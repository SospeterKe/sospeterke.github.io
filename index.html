Courses we offer

Some of the courses offered are listed below

Intermediate

The Intermediate Courses are taught at the Government or Private or Cooperative Junior Colleges for two years. These Courses are followed by prestigious State level Entrance Tests for admission to the various Degree Colleges offering Professional or general education.

Degree

Degree Course means a course of study at a recognised higher education institution of at least three years full-time duration, or its part-time equivalent.

Postgraduate

Postgraduate education involves learning and studying for academic or professional degrees, academic or professional certificates, academic or professional diplomas, or other qualifications for which a first or bachelor's degree generally is required, and it is normally considered to be part of higher education
